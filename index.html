Ethnic and Religious Conflict
Resolution and Peacebuilding

2022 International Conference on

To increase our understanding of ethnic, racial and religious conflicts in countries around the world, the conference will consider submissions from multidisciplinary fields of study and practice. Qualitative, quantitative or mixed methods research studies from university scholars and researchers are accepted. Case studies, lessons learned, success stories, policy analysis or design, and best practices from policy makers, practitioners, religious leaders and indigenous peoples are also accepted

September 28-September 29, 2022 Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

@ African union.

Sessions

Politics & Governance

In the face of existing and emerging geopolitical contexts such as the consolidation of right-wing nationalism, how does one reimagine Politics and Governance at this point in time?

Socioeconomic Transformation

By identifying the scope of development that we are working towards in Africa, how can we secure our socioeconomic future?

Culture & Identity

Who are we, as Africans? What is essential to being ‘African’ that makes us African? How has being ‘African’ evolved through the years?
